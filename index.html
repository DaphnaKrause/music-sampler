Taylor Swift: Her Music Evolution

Taylor Swift performing on her Reputation Stadium Tour in May 2018. From UltimateWarrior13 [CC BY-SA 4.0], from Wikimedia Commons.

Taylor Swift began her career as a country music star moving into the pop genre in more recent years. Each album of hers offers a different sound as her music taste evolve. With Swift you know you will be getting quality music with each passing album. A lot of her earlier work focused on relationships and love. In addition, her music videos are large productions giving her a greater platform to showcase her work.

Love Story, from Taylor Swifts Fearless Album (2008)
